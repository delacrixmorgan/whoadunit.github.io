What does your MP do?

Your MP votes on national laws at Parliament in Kuala Lumpur. They raise questions directly to ministers and shape policy that affects every Malaysian — not just your area.

Sample email
Stuck? Borrow this template.
  1. 01 · Greeting
    Address the rep with their title and name.
    Dear YB Ting Tong Piao,
  2. 02 · The issue
    State the topic and your concern in one paragraph.
    I am writing concerning the proposed development of the PJ – Cheras highway that will start in early 2016. The news that the Taman Hometown park will be demolished to accommodate this new highway is upsetting to me and many residents of Taman Hometown.
  3. 03 · Your story
    Make it personal. Why does this matter to you?
    I have lived in the Taman Hometown area for the past 15 years and the park has become a personal landmark for many residents. It's a meeting point for senior citizens as well as a popular place for young families and their children to play and exercise. The park is also the last green lung in the area and home to small wildlife such as monkeys and wild boars, so it is a shame for residents to lose their last connection to nature within the city.
  4. 04 · Your ask
    Be specific about what you want them to do.
    Your party has previously spoken against the development of this highway, and I hope you will join them in opposing this project.
  5. 05 · Sign off
    Optional thanks, questions, or a request for a reply. Always sign your name.
    I also want to thank you for your commitment and dedication to the Hometown Jaya constituency so far. What is your personal stand regarding this development? What steps will you or your party be taking to prevent this development? A reply to these key questions will be highly appreciated. Sincerely, [Your name]
